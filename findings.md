# WCAG 2.2 Findings

## 1. Perceivable

### 1.1 Text Alternatives  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** All images have descriptive alt text that conveys the purpose of the image.  
**Severity Rating:** Low

### 1.2 Time-based Media  
**Status:** Fail  
**Explanation:** Videos do not have captions or transcripts, making them inaccessible to users who are deaf or hard of hearing.  
**Severity Rating:** High

### 1.3 Adaptable  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** Content can be presented in different ways without losing information.  
**Severity Rating:** Medium

### 1.4 Distinguishable  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** Text contrast ratios meet the minimum requirements, ensuring readability.  
**Severity Rating:** Low

## 2. Operable

### 2.1 Keyboard Accessible  
**Status:** Fail  
**Explanation:** Some navigation elements are not focusable using a keyboard, which limits accessibility.  
**Severity Rating:** High

### 2.2 Enough Time  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** Users have enough time to read and use content.  
**Severity Rating:** Low

### 2.3 Seizures and Physical Reactions  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** No content flashes unnecessarily, avoiding risks for users with seizure disorders.  
**Severity Rating:** Low

### 2.4 Navigable  
**Status:** Fail  
**Explanation:** Many links are poorly labeled, making navigation confusing for screen reader users.  
**Severity Rating:** Medium

## 3. Understandable

### 3.1 Readable  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** Text is easy to read with simple language and structures.  
**Severity Rating:** Low

### 3.2 Predictable  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** Consistent navigation and layout are maintained throughout the site.  
**Severity Rating:** Low

### 3.3 Input Assistance  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** Input errors are clearly identified, and suggestions for corrections are provided.  
**Severity Rating:** Medium

## 4. Robust

### 4.1 Compatible  
**Status:** Pass  
**Explanation:** The website works well with current and future assistive technologies.  
**Severity Rating:** Low
